I spent my recent projects and term breaks diving deep into RAG pipelines and multi-agent orchestration to understand AI from first principles.

After my first internship, I realized I wanted to specialize in agentic systems rather than being a generalist engineer. Multi-agent orchestration feels like the richest area—it’s how we solve complex, real-world problems like urban monitoring or automated onboarding, and machines that can coordinate specialized tasks are the future of the industry.
